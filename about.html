About

  • Project Love & Feed

    Turmerican's first baby step towards community giving, project "Love & Feed" provides free lunch on Tuesdays, Wednesdays, and Thursdays for senior women(65 and above) in need.

  • See Through Kitchen Concept

    Watch your food get made right in front of you, entertain yourself and see our commitment to quality

  • Create Your Own Stir Fries

    Pick your own favorite veggies and oil and customize your own fried rice/noodles

  • Specialty Menu

    Delivering vegetarian/vegan, organic,non-genetically modified, unrefined foods as much as possible

  • Authentic South Indian

    Authentic and very traditional items like Idiappam, Kuzhipaniyaram, Madurai, Jigarthanda offeed in the menu are found rarely in America

  • Soups & Sandwich

    Our soups incorporate fresh produce, and all natural/organic ingredients. We offer vegan burgers and organic bread with multigrain and gluten free options to meet your various needs.

  • Ancient Grains, Legumes & Seeds

    We incorporate ancient grains, legumes, and seeds like quinoa, forbidden rice, blackwildrice, amranth, spelt, millet, barley, and horsegram. We hope this provides you an innovative, nutritious, and delicious meal.

  • Fresh Fries and Chips

    Our fries are fried in unrefined, cold pressed coconut oil. Explore hot and fresh varieties of chips such as plantain, tapioca, banana, bittermelon, and many more.
